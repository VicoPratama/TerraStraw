About Us

TerraWrap is a bubble wrap replacement that uses eco-friendly paper-based materials. Made from recycled paper material, TerraWrap actively supports the circular economy and sustainable development.
Therefore, TerraWrap not only provides eco-friendly packaging solutions but also makes a diversified products, Wheat Based Material Straw. Hopefully, Terra Wrap could give significant contribution in maintaining environmental sustainability in Indonesia.
